About ChemPhoPro 2.0

ChemPhoPro provides a compendium of results and related information obtained from chemical phosphoproteomics experiments in the lab of Pedro Cutillas at Barts Cancer Institute, Queen Mary University of London.

Contributors: Maruan Hijazi, Ryan Smith, Vinothini Rajeeve, Conrad Bessant and Pedro R. Cutillas.
